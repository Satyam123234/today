Step-by-Step Activation Guide

TV Service Setup Instructions

Follow these easy steps to activate and set up your TV service effortlessly.

1

Unbox & Power Up

  • • Carefully unbox the TV and connect it to a power source
  • • Ensure that the TV is connected to your home Wi-Fi network or Ethernet
  • • Turn on the TV and follow the initial setup instructions on the screen
2

Install the App

  • • Open the TV's app store or content marketplace
  • • Search for your TV service provider’s official app
  • • Download and install the app on your TV
3

Open & Sign In

  • • Launch the installed app on your TV
  • • Click "Sign In" or "Activate Device"
  • • Wait for the activation screen to show up
4

Retrieve the Activation Code

  • • Note the unique activation code shown on the TV screen
  • • Activation codes typically consist of 6-8 alphanumeric characters
  • • Keep in mind that codes expire in 10-15 minutes, so act quickly!
5

Activate Your Service

On Your Computer/Phone:

  • • Visit the activation website of your service provider
  • • Log in with your account credentials
  • • Enter the activation code when prompted

On Your TV:

  • • Wait for confirmation once the code is entered
  • • Your TV should automatically refresh and load your service
  • • Enjoy your newly activated TV service!

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

  • • Code not working? Refresh your TV screen for a new code
  • • Activation stuck? Restart both your TV and router
  • • App not loading? Check for updates in your TV's app store

Need Help?

If you're still having trouble setting up your service, contact customer support or visit the support page on your provider's website.
